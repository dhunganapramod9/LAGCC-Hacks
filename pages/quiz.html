Which Career or Major is for you?

1. Why are you interested in taking a career test? Choose the option that best describes your motivation
2. What are you looking for in your ideal career? Think about what matters most to you in your career
3. What are some of your strengths? Choose the set of strengths that best describes you
4. What are some of your hobbies/interests? Select your primary hobby or interest
5. How much time are you willing to spend in college? Consider your educational timeline preferences
6. What is your preference on working independently? Think about your preferred work environment
7. What do you hope to achieve with your career? Consider your long-term career goals
8. How would your perfect workplace be structured? Think about your ideal work setting
9. How much time would you like to spend daily on your job? Consider your preferred work schedule
